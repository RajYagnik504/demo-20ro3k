About Tipsy Topsy

At Tipsy Topsy, we believe food is more than just nourishment — it is an experience. Every dish is crafted fresh daily using hand-picked ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Located at E-51-52-53, Kanaknidhi Shopping Complex, Surat, we are proud to serve the people of Surat with authentic North Indian flavours. Jain options available on all dishes.
